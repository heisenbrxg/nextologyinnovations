Our experts are able to answer all your questions

  • What services do you offer?

    Nextology Innovation specializes in Enterprise Cloud Solutions, Artificial Intelligence (AI) Development, Mobile & Web Applications, and full-scale Digital Transformation services.

  • How do I get started with a project?

    You can start by clicking the "Book a Call" button to schedule a free consultation. We'll discuss your requirements, scope out a timeline, and provide a detailed proposal.

  • What industries do you serve?

    We work with a diverse range of industries including healthcare, finance, retail, and manufacturing, providing tailored software solutions to meet sector-specific challenges.

  • Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

    Yes! We offer comprehensive post-launch support and maintenance to ensure your applications remain secure, optimized, and up-to-date with the latest technologies.

  • How long does a typical project take?

    Project timelines vary based on complexity, but a standard web or mobile application typically takes between 3 to 6 months from discovery to successful deployment.

  • Where is Nextology Innovation located?

    We are headquartered in Chennai, India, at 172A, Bharathipuram, Chrompet, but we serve clients globally and offer fully remote collaboration models.

  • What is your company's mission?

    Our mission is to harness the power of emerging technologies, particularly Artificial Intelligence, to solve complex business challenges and drive digital transformation for our clients.

  • How long have you been in business?

    Nextology Innovation has grown rapidly as a premier technology partner, bringing together a team of seasoned experts with decades of combined experience in the tech industry.

  • Do you use an agile development process?

    Absolutely. We follow an iterative Agile methodology, ensuring regular updates, transparent communication, and the flexibility to adapt to changing requirements.

  • What sets Nextology apart from other agencies?

    We combine enterprise-grade engineering with a startup-like agility. We focus not just on writing code, but on delivering measurable business impact and exceptional user experiences.

  • What kind of AI solutions do you build?

    We build everything from custom Large Language Models (LLMs) and advanced natural language chatbots to predictive analytics tools and automated workflow systems.

  • Do I need a massive dataset to use AI?

    Not necessarily. While deep learning models require significant data, we can leverage pre-trained models (like OpenAI or Hugging Face) and fine-tune them on your specific, smaller datasets.

  • Will AI integrate with my existing software?

    Yes. We design our AI solutions with API-first architectures so they can seamlessly integrate into your current CRM, ERP, or custom software stack without disruption.

  • How do you ensure the AI is secure and private?

    Security is our top priority. We use secure cloud infrastructure, deploy models in isolated environments, and enforce strict data anonymization protocols to protect your proprietary information.

  • Can AI automate our customer support?

    Definitely. We develop intelligent chatbots that can handle up to 80% of routine customer inquiries 24/7, escalating only complex issues to human agents.

  • Who will be working on my project?

    Your project will be handled by a dedicated squad consisting of a Project Manager, UI/UX Designer, Lead Developer, and QA Specialist, all tailored to your technology stack.

  • Can I communicate directly with the developers?

    Yes, we believe in transparent communication. You will have direct access to your project team via Slack, Teams, or regular sprint review video calls.

  • What is the expertise level of your engineers?

    Our team is composed of mid-to-senior level engineers with deep expertise in modern technologies like React, Node.js, Python, AWS, and advanced AI frameworks.

  • Are your team members in-house or outsourced?

    Our core team consists of trusted, full-time in-house experts based in our Chennai office, ensuring consistent quality, accountability, and seamless collaboration.

  • How does the team handle time zone differences?

    We are highly flexible and establish overlapping availability windows. We ensure regular sync-ups at times convenient for your primary stakeholders, regardless of your global location.
